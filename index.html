Youtube Thumbnail Grabber

The Easiest way to download YouTube thumbnails effortlessly!

How to use?

First step

Find a video on YouTube for which you would like to download its thumbnail.

Second step

After obtaining the link, paste it into the input box, and click "Download Thumbnail"

Final step

You're all set! Simply click the download button corresponding to the desired resolution.
